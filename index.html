About Me

Hi! I am Teresa, an incoming PhD student at UCL’s Centre for Data Intensive Science. My research is about galactic archaeology, using data-intensive approaches to understand the evolution of the Milky Way.

Before starting my PhD, I completed my BSc in Theoretical Physics at UCL and Part III (MAst in Theoretical Physics) at the University of Cambridge.
