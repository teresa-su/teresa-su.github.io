Projects [ 3 ]
  • Stellar ages with Simulation-Based Inference

With the advent of large spectroscopic datasets, I am using SBI for efficient isochrone fitting to determine stellar ages for millions of stars. My current focus is the DESI Milky Way Survey, with plans to extend the pipeline to Gaia XP spectra.

  • Generative Latent Optimisation for cool stars

Cool stars like M-dwarfs are challenging for stellar characterisation. I plan to use GLO to infer stellar parameters for cool stars, learning a low-dimensional latent representation for their spectra that enable more robust parameter estimation.
